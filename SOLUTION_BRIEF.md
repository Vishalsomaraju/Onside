# Smart Stadiums & Tournament Operations: Solution Brief

## Target User
Ticketed fan at a FIFA World Cup 2026 stadium.

## Problem Statement
First-time and repeat match-day attendees at FIFA World Cup 2026 stadiums routinely get lost, miss kickoff, and can't find accessible or language-appropriate help.

## Core Workflow
A single-workflow tool where a fan asks "how do I get to X" (a gate, seat block, restroom, food stand, accessible exit) in their own words and language. The system computes a deterministic, step-by-step route and the AI explains the route in natural language, personalized to the user's language and accessibility needs, aware of simulated real-time congestion.
